CUP OF JOE




Cup of Joe is a up and rising Filipino band who is famous for their songs. The band is composed
of six members. Edgar Gian Bernardino and Raphaell Ridao as vocalists, pianist Vixen “Xen” Gareza, lead
guitarist Antonio Gabriel “Gab” Fernandez, bassist Raphael “Seve” Severino and rhythm guitarist Clint Joules “CJ” Fernandez.

The band’s unique name, Cup of Joe, is a slang word for a cup of coffee. And just like coffee, they wanted to produce different blends of music for people to listen to, especially when they need that refreshing boost to get through the day.

Members are fresh out of senior high where they formed the band just last November 2018. According to Cup of Joe, they’re still exploring their sound as a band but their catchy pop songs already caught the attention of listeners. Their song “Nag-iisang Muli” recently won as the Best Regional Song in MOR Awards 2019.
